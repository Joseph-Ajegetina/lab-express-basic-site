Early Life and Education

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. From an early age, he displayed an exceptional aptitude for science and technology. Musk attended the University of Pretoria before transferring to the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned degrees in physics and economics.
